6. The present age of a father is 3 years more than three times the age of his son. Three yeras hence, father's age will be 10 years more than twice the age of the son. Find the present age of the father.

A. 27 years✘

B. 30 years✘

C. 36 years✘

D. 33 years✔

x

 

Option: D

Explanation

Consider son's present age be X years

The present age of a father is 3 years more than three times the age of his son. i.e) father's present age be (3X + 3) Years

Three yeras hence, father's age will be 10 years more than twice the age of the son

i.e) (3X + 3 + 3) = 2(X + 3) + 10

3X + 6 = 2X + 16

X = 10

i.e) father's present age = (3X + 3) = ((3 * 10) + 3) = 33 Years

✉ Answer

7. A person was asked to state his age in years. His reply was, "Take my age three years hence, multiply it by 3 and then subtract three times my age three years ago and you will know how old i am." What was the age of the person?

A. 18 years✔

B. 20 years✘

C. 24 years✘

D. 32 years✘

x

 

Option: A

Explanation

consider the present age of the person be X years

from the qn we know that 3(X + 3) - 3(X - 3) = X

(3X + 9) - (3X - 9) = X

3X + 9 - 3X + 9 = X

i.e) X = 18

Age of the person is 18 years

✉ Answer
