A. 331✘

B. 383✔

C. 111✘

D. 242✘

x

 

Option: B

Explanation

Ans:383

Pattern is 2nd digit = 1st digit * 3rd digit

i.e) In the number 383, 8 ≠ 3 * 3

✉ Answer
